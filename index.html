We love good coffee

Since 1989, Consciious Coffee has built its reputation roasting its own exclusively heirloom, organic coffees and offering hand-selected fine teas. Only a handful of the world's shade-grown, certified organic coffee beans can produce the flavor, richness, and aroma that make our's world-renowned coffees stand out from traditionally grown coffees. Visit our locations in Oslo, Bergen and Trondheim.

Why buy organic?

Coffee is the heaviest chemically treated food commodity in the world, and the coffee industry is wrought with environmental destruction, deforestation, and social oppression. In support of passionate sustainable organic coffee farmers – we has sought to bring the greatest coffee to our customers – a coffee that is not only superb, but is also healthier for the people who grow it, who drink it, and for the world.

Who are we

Conscious Coffee is widely considered the first coffee roasting company in Norway to offer exclusively organically grown, heirloom coffees. Each season we choose the very finest beans from our farmers – carefully selecting only a small percentage of the crop representing the coffee's finest seasonal flavor & characteristics.
